I really expected to be blown away by reading this after all the praise that it got from all over the internet but no. It was a really down to the ground and realistic story of a marvel superhero similar to no other, but... it... got...boring. No big surprises, no complex plot, nothing at all. I really enjoyed tho the unique way that... There is a lot that's good about this. The art, of course. I love how Aja lays out his panels. The simplistic lines and coloring of some issues call up an indie feel, while he dives back into a retro style that called to mind comics from the Silver Age for other issues.
