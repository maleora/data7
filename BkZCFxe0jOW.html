More About Exile

I only made it thirty pages or so before giving up. I liked the premise enough to pull the book off the shelf, but was quickly tired of the very spoiled heroine who couldn't even light a fire and was a spoiled brat on top of it. I want to give this 2 1/2 stars. It was ok, but nothing special. The main character was rather annoying, almost never learning from her mistakes and was stubborn to a fault. It wasn't a terrible novel, but it wasn't great either. Maybe slightly below average. I always loved this book and all the author's other stories. I wish she had written more books. Her characters are approachable and yet also wonderfully foreign. The land is just obscure enough to not be ours but also familiar enough to make it believable. I love her humor and her characterization.
