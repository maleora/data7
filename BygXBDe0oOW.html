More About The Babe and the Baron

I have enjoyed all of Carola Dunns regency books I have read so far. They are a nice, quick, easy read and they have a Jane Austen feel to them. This doesn't really phase Laura because she is used to taking care of herself, sewing and embroidery for the locals and she didn't think her husband actually loved her....
