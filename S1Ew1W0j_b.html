Nonfiction. Literary History & Criticism. For over a quarter century, many readers have agreed with legendary publisher Jack McClelland who said, "I have never before encountered a book journal as engaging as BC BookWorld." Here in THE BEST OF BC BOOKWORLD is a sampling of articles that entertain, enlighten, educate and provoke. There is something for everyone here: from early explorers of the Pacific Slope, to essays about literature, to BC authors who have written about the Holocaust.
