More About I Am an Islander

I bought an armful of books about Prince Edward Island on the second-to-last day of vacation there, when I didn't want to leave. I thought they would remind me of what it felt like to be there. This book is light, fun, amusing, but probably not of particular interest to anyone who doesn't love PEI. Regional humour at it's best, this book provided some gentle chuckles to laugh out loud moments. A perfect book for Islanders or those puzzled by our customs. Ledwell is consistently witty and gentle in his humour. Cute, lots of laughs, and most that I could relate to.
